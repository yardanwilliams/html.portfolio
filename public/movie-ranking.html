The best movies according to Yardan

My top 3 greatest films of all time

Dune

It stays true to the novels and I love the sci-fi aspects of it. The fight scenes are amazing and action packed, the plot is good and the power/magic system is wonderous and piques your curiosity.

The Hobbit trilogy

The Hobbit is a fun and adventurous film with an amazing plot. It is the prequel to the Lord Of The Rings and has a ton of world building and lore.

Alien movies

As a sci-fi fan, the Alien movies stand out to me as some of the greatest in it's genre. It is freaky, gory and will have you on the edge of your seat most of the time. Plot is good and it is also action packed
